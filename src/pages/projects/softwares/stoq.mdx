**Stoq Tecnologia** develops retail software for Brazilian businesses, offering point-of-sale, store management and digital commerce products such as Stoq POS, stoq.link and stoq.shop.

https://stoq.com.br/

## Experience
Here is a selection of products I have contributed to as a full-stack developer, with a primary focus on Python, React and PostgreSQL.

### StoqPDV
An open-source retail management system for running store operations, sales and business administration. It includes stoq.link, a cloud extension that adds advanced features such as reports, encrypted backups and data synchronization across stores.

<YouTube id="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EK0Gqqkf6hE" />

### Stoq Shop
A mobile shopping experience that puts the store in the customer’s phone, making it easy to browse products, get recommendations and pay without lines.

### SmartPOS
